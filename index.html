About Caleb CGPA Advisor

What This Tool Does

The Caleb University CGPA Advisor helps students:

  • Calculate current and projected CGPA based on Caleb University's grading system
  • Determine the grades needed to achieve target CGPA
  • Get personalized study recommendations
  • Plan study time effectively

How CGPA is Calculated

CGPA = Σ (Course Credit Units × Grade Points) ÷ Total Credit Units Taken

Example: If you took 3 courses (4 units, 3 units, 2 units) with grades A (5), B (4), and C (3):
CGPA = [(4×5) + (3×4) + (2×3)] ÷ (4+3+2) = 38 ÷ 9 = 4.22
